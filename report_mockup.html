Daily Revenue & Spend

Source: channel_daily  ·  Metrics: revenue_usd, spend_usd
Tracks how much revenue was generated vs. how much was spent on paid campaigns each month. A widening gap between the two lines means improving efficiency — you're earning more per dollar spent.

Conversion Rate by Channel

Source: channel_daily  ·  Metric: cvr
Shows the % of sessions that resulted in a purchase, broken down by traffic source. Higher CVR channels are more efficient at turning visitors into buyers — useful for deciding where to invest budget.
Price Elasticity

CVR Lift by Discount Tier & Channel

Source: discount_elasticity  ·  Metric: cvr_lift_pct  ·  Dimension: discount_bucket
Compares conversion rates at each discount level against the 0% (no discount) baseline. Taller bars mean the discount had a bigger uplift effect on purchases. Email responds most strongly — a 25% discount nearly doubles its CVR.

Implied Elasticity by Channel

Source: discount_elasticity  ·  Metric: implied_elasticity
Measures how sensitive each channel's demand is to price changes. A value of –2.1 means a 10% discount drives a ~21% increase in conversions. Channels closer to 0 are inelastic — discounts there have little effect.
